THEO Engine

AI built for
African languages

THEO is not a generic language model. It is purpose-built for Medumba — trained on 50 years of CEPOM publications, native speaker recordings, and the 14-dimensional cultural fingerprint of the Ndé people.

🎵
Tonal language intelligence
Medumba is a tonal language. THEO understands pitch, tone, and phoneme distinctions that generic AI models miss entirely.
🗺
Village-level cultural awareness
THEO tracks 14 cultural dimensions per village — from oral traditions to sacred knowledge — so the AI respects and reflects each community's specific heritage.
🔒
Tiered access for sacred knowledge
Not all cultural knowledge is for everyone. THEO has a built-in access protocol — public, community, elders-only — that respects traditional governance.
📈
Language vitality tracking
THEO monitors real-time language health — speaker demographics, transmission rates, vocabulary erosion — and generates fieldwork priorities for preservation teams.

Heritage archive

50 years of
Medumba
knowledge.

CEPOM — the Comité de Langue pour l'Étude et la Production des Œuvres Bamiléké-Medumba — has been documenting the Medumba language since 1973. Their 80+ publications are now digitized, searchable, and accessible to every learner on Medumba.ai.

Academic researchers and linguistic partners access this corpus freely. This knowledge belongs to the community — we simply make it available to the world.
